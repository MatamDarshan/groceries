Rolls-Royce La Rose Noire Droptail

The Rolls-Royce La Rose Noire Droptail is the world's most expensive car, priced at $30 million. This two-seater supercar features a removable hardtop, a twin-turbo 6.75-liter V-12 engine producing 563 bhp, and a body made of carbon, steel, and aluminum. Inspired by the petals of the Black Baccara rose, its body color transitions when viewed from different angles. The interior is minimalistic with wood craftsmanship and dark red leather seats. This car is a true masterpiece of automotive artistry.
